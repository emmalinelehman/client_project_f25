Career Outcomes

Career Outcomes Overview

The University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI) prepares students for a wide range of career paths in the information field. Our graduates are equipped with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in various roles, including data analysis, user experience design, information architecture, and more.

Job Placement Statistics

UMSI boasts a strong track record of job placement for its graduates. According to recent data, over 90% of our graduates secure employment within six months of graduation, with many finding positions at top companies in the tech industry.

Alumni Success Stories

Our alumni have gone on to achieve great success in their careers. From leading innovative projects at major corporations to starting their own ventures, UMSI graduates are making a significant impact in the information field.
