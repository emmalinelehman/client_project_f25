Career Exploration

Your guide to exploring career paths and opportunities at UMSI

Career Outcomes Overview

The University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI) prepares students for a wide range of career paths in the information field. Our graduates are equipped with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in various roles, including data analysis, user experience design, information architecture, and more.

Job Placement Statistics

UMSI boasts a strong track record of job placement for its graduates. According to recent data, over 90% of our graduates secure employment within six months of graduation, with many finding positions at top companies in the tech industry. For detailed statistics, visit our Career Outcomes page, or view our Employment Report.

Attend an Event!

Participating in career events is a great way to network and learn about job opportunities. Check the UMSI events calendar for upcoming workshops, guest lectures, and networking events.

Career Exploration Resources

Discover a variety of resources to help you explore career paths, research industries, and identify potential job opportunities. UMSI offers career guides, industry panels, and online tools to support your professional journey.

  • Career Guides: Access detailed guides on different fields such as data science, UX design, information policy, and more. These guides provide insights into required skills, typical job roles, and industry trends.
  • Industry Panels: Attend panel discussions featuring professionals from diverse industries. Learn about their career journeys, challenges, and advice for students entering the workforce.
  • Online Tools: Utilize platforms like LinkedIn, Handshake, and Glassdoor to research companies, connect with alumni, and find job postings tailored to your interests.
  • Career Counseling: Schedule one-on-one appointments with UMSI career advisors for personalized guidance on career exploration, resume building, and interview preparation.
  • Workshops & Webinars: Participate in skill-building workshops and webinars covering topics such as networking, personal branding, and job search strategies.
